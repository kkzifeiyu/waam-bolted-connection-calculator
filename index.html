METHODOLOGY, SCOPE AND REFERENCES

Technical Basis of the Calculator

The calculator evaluates the inner plate of a single-bolt, non-preloaded, double-shear bearing-type connection made from WAAM austenitic or duplex stainless steel.

01

Scope

Included

  • Standard circular bolt holes
  • Direction-dependent fy and fu
  • Net-section rupture (NSR)
  • Shear-out failure (SOF)
  • Bearing failure (BF)

Outside the scope

  • Bolt shear, tension or combined failure
  • Friction, slip resistance and bolt pretension
  • Outer cover plate failure
  • Multiple-bolt and staggered-hole effects
  • Fatigue, seismic, fire and corrosion design

Bolt resistance and outer cover plate resistance must be verified separately.
